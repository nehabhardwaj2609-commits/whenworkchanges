Participate in the research

You spend all day shaping the narrative.
I want to hear yours.

What's in it for you

A chance to put the focus
on your own experience.

As a communications professional, your job is to clarify the message for everyone else. This research is a chance to put the focus on your own experience. By participating, you get:

A structured debrief
60 to 90 minutes dedicated entirely to your career journey. A confidential space to step back, look at the big picture, and process what this technological shift means for you personally.
Real influence
Your insights will go directly toward building a practical framework designed to help coaches, leaders, and organisations actually support people through AI-driven change. You are helping build something that doesn't exist yet.
Who I need to speak with

You are the right person
if this sounds like you.

  • You have 10 or more years of experience in communications, PR, content, editorial, or policy writing.
  • You are based in Aotearoa New Zealand.
  • Your day-to-day work has shifted because of AI, whether that means you are now editing machine drafts, your role has been restructured, or you're simply asking: where does my expertise fit now?
  • You care deeply about the craft and the future of the profession.
What participation looks like

Simple, safe,
and entirely on your terms.

01
One conversation
We will meet for 60 to 90 minutes, via video call or in person. We will use a visual journey map together to help plot your experience.
02
Total control
This is your story, and you own your data. You decide exactly what to share, and you can withdraw from the study at any time.
03
Strictly confidential
This study is fully ethics-approved by AcademyEX. Neither you nor your employer will ever be identified in the research.
04
Protected data
Your raw data and interview transcripts will be stored securely and will never be fed into cloud-based AI models.

Ready to share your story?

You don't need a CV or a formal statement. Just a quick note to say you're interested and I'll take it from there.
