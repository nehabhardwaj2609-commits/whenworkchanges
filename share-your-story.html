Participate in the research

You spend all day shaping the narrative.
I want to hear yours.

Is this you?

  • 10 or more years in communications, PR, content strategy, editorial, or policy writing.
  • Based in New Zealand.
  • Passionate about the communications craft.
  • Your role has changed or is changing, whether that is a restructure or parts of what you do are simply evolving.

You don't need to have lost your job. You just need to be living inside the change.

What's involved

One conversation. 60 to 90 minutes. Video call or in person.

It's a deep dive into your career journey, a chance to reflect on how your work has changed, what that's felt like, and where you are now. You share as much or as little as you want. There are no right answers.

Everything is completely confidential. You and where you work, or have worked, are never identified in the research. Ethics-approved by AcademyEX. You can withdraw at any time, no questions asked.

What you get out of it

A space to say it out loud. And something useful at the end of it.

A dedicated hour to step back and process what this shift has actually meant for you, not your LinkedIn version of it, the real one.

Your story also feeds directly into building a practical sense-making framework, grounded in the real experiences of communications professionals. Something that helps make sense of what's shifting, what's working, what's helping, and what's getting in the way. For you, and for others going through the same thing.
